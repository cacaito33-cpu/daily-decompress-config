# Privacy Policy — Daily Decompress

_Last updated: June 18, 2026_

Daily Decompress is built around a simple promise: **your reflections are yours,
and they stay on your device.**

## The short version

Daily Decompress does **not collect, transmit, sell, or share any of your personal
data.** There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking. Your
voice recordings, transcripts, and reflections are created and stored entirely on
your iPhone.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** We do not operate a server that receives your data, and we do not
have access to your journal entries, recordings, or reflections.

## How your information is used (all on-device)

- **Microphone:** When you choose to record, audio is captured and saved locally
  on your device so you can play it back later.
- **Speech recognition:** Your recordings are transcribed **on your device** using
  Apple's on-device speech recognition. The transcript is stored locally.
- **On-device intelligence:** Reflections and mood signals are generated **on your
  device** using Apple's on-device models. Your entry text is not sent to us or to
  any third party for this.

## iCloud sync (optional)

If you enable iCloud sync, your journal is stored in **your own iCloud account**
through Apple's iCloud/CloudKit so it can sync across your devices. This data lives
in your personal iCloud and is governed by
[Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). **We cannot access
it.** You can turn sync off at any time.

## App configuration

The app downloads a small, public configuration file at launch to enable or
disable features (for example, to pause a feature for maintenance). This is a
plain download of a public file and **sends none of your personal data.**

## Your control over your data

- Delete any entry directly in the app.
- Deleting the app removes all locally stored entries and recordings from your
  device. (If iCloud sync is on, you can manage that copy in your iCloud settings.)

## Children

Daily Decompress is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data
from anyone.

## Not medical advice

Daily Decompress is a general wellness and self-reflection tool. It is not therapy,
diagnosis, or a medical device.

## Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated"
